# About This Initiative

This site is the public front door for the Reconstruction-Grade eDiscovery Standard. It is intended to make the standard easier to evaluate, navigate, and participate in without replacing the underlying source-of-truth model.

## What this website contains

- A public-facing overview for lawyers, legal operations teams, enterprise buyers, analysts, and working-group participants
- The full canonical standard chapters and appendices
- Practical, non-normative toolkit resources for evaluation and implementation
- Governance, participation, and version-transparency materials

## What remains canonical

The authoritative text remains the Markdown documentation in the public repository:

- [Canonical Markdown source](https://github.com/cloudficient/reconstruction-grade-ediscovery-standard/tree/main/standard)
- [Front matter and executive summary](../front-matter.md)
- [Version history](https://github.com/cloudficient/reconstruction-grade-ediscovery-standard/blob/main/CHANGELOG.md)

## Institutional boundaries

- The standard is not legal advice.
- The website is not a product landing page.
- The draft does not itself create legal duties.
- The framework does not imply that all non-conforming workflows are per se deficient.
- The standard is intended to define measurable preservation and reconstruction targets, including bounded evidence handling where perfect determinism is unavailable.
